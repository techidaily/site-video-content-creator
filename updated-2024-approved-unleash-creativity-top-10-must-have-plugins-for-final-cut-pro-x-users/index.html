Recently, they created Universe, which allows you to access to a wealth of effects, for a very reasonable fee each month. Price: Prices range from free to several hundred dollars, depending upon the package.

9 Intelligent Assistance

Price : Starting at $9.99, depending upon the package

They provide tools that help with logging footage, generating production reports, and converting projects between versions. Not everyone needs these tools, but when you do, theres nothing better. There are many styles you can choose from the cool Final Cut Pro plugins. If you want to make your titles powerful, this one the best place to go.
