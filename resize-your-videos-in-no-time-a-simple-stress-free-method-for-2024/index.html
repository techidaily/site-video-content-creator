Part 2 Difference Between Aspect Ratio and Resolution

Aspect ratio and resolution are interrelated and go hand-in-hand. For a certain aspect ratio, there is several counterparts‚Äô resolution that can be used. While the aspect ratio defines the width and the height relationship, the resolution is the number of pixels on the screen.
