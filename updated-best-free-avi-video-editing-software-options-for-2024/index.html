3. VirtualDub

Its geared towards processing AVI files and able to do a variety of pre and post-processing operations on your video. Reminder: while installing, it will write some preferences to the Registry.

Some of its outstanding features include video capture and import, GPU- accelerated real-time effects, color correction, autosave and media management, etc.

5. ZS4 Video Editor

ZS4 Video Editor is another free video editing tool with over 150 built-in video effects including excellent color and chroma keying.
